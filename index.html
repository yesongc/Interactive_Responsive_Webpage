HOSTILE WORK ENVIROMENT


is demonstrated by severe or pervasive conduct that creates a work environment a reasonable person would consider intimidating, hostile, or abusive.
Bullying
Workplace bullying is defined as the
repeated abuse of one or more persons
by one or more perpetrators that:
  • Is done with malice
  • Is threatening, humiliating or intimidating
  • Is verbally or physically abusive
  • Interferes with the person’s ability to get his or her work done
  • Is unrelated to the Company’s legitimate business interests
